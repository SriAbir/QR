Usage & Care Instructions

  • Place synthetic garments inside the MicroCatch bag.
  • Zip fully before placing in machine.
  • Wash normally using eco-friendly detergent.
  • Post-Wash Microfiber Removal:
    • After washing, turn the bag inside out gently.
    • Shake out trapped microfibers over a trash bin (preferably lined)
    • Do not rinse fibers down the drain, dispose the fibers in general household waste or compost bin if allowed
    • Shake out trapped microfibers over a trash bin (preferably lined)
  • Reapplying the Cornstarch Coating (Every 5–10 washes):
    • Mix 1 tablespoon of cornstarch with 1 liter of warm water.
    • Soak the bag completely for 15–30 minutes.
    • Air-dry fully before reuse (avoid dryers)

End of Life Instruction

Composting: Cut or shred the bag for faster composting.

Add to industrial or home compost with food or yard waste.

Entire bag is 100% compostable: no disassembly required.

Landfill Disposal: If composting is unavailable, MicroCatch can safely go into general waste. Will naturally biodegrade over several years, unlike plastic alternatives.,
